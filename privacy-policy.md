# Wiralgram – Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** Upon public release  
**Version:** 1.0

Wiralgram (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and services.

By accessing or using Wiralgram, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the platform:

### 1.1 Account Information

- Email address
- Username
- Date of Birth (DOB)

### 1.2 Optional Verification Information

- Identity documents (only if you voluntarily choose to verify your account)

### 1.3 Usage & Device Information

- Content you create (posts, interactions, views)
- Log data (IP address, device type, app activity, timestamps)
- Device identifiers for security and fraud prevention

### 1.4 Media Access

- When you upload a video or update your avatar, you choose the photo or video manually through your device's system picker.
- The app only accesses the specific media file you select for upload or avatar update.
- We do not scan your device's media library.
- We do not access your full gallery.
- We do not upload any media unless you choose it yourself.

### 1.5 Location

- We do **not** collect your device’s GPS, precise location, or real-time whereabouts.
- We do **not** request access to your phone’s location permission for this purpose.
- If you optionally add a place name to a post or clip, that is **text you enter or pick from search suggestions** — not coordinates from your device.
- Routine log data (such as IP address in section 1.3) is used for security and operations only; it is not used as GPS-style location tracking.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

- Active account data → retained while your account is active

### Legal Retention Requirement

Certain financial, payout, tax, transaction, security, fraud-prevention, and compliance records may be retained for a longer period as required or permitted under Indian law.

## 8. Account Deletion (“Right to Erasure”)

You may delete your account at any time.

Upon deletion:

- Personal data such as profile information, content, identity documents, and DOB is generally deleted or de-identified within **30 days**
- Legally required financial, payout, tax, transaction, security, fraud-prevention, dispute, audit, and compliance records may be retained as required or permitted by law
- Backup copies may remain for a limited period as part of routine backup systems, but are not used for active service purposes and are deleted or overwritten according to our backup retention practices
